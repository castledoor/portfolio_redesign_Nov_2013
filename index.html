ABOUT

I’m Brittany, a web developer / designer based in Portland, OR

My background is in architecture, but in the last year I have switched gears into web development and through this journey I have realized that the two industries are not as different as one might think. At the core of them both is my passion for design and technology. I am a curious soul and can't get enough of learning. Documentaries and tech magazines can often be the source of my procrastination. I enjoy building things both physical and digital, and can be a real tinkerer for better or worse. I love to travel and experience new places. I am driven by my biggest motivator in life, and that would be my obsession with castles. I think they are beautiful, hold great history and stories... I will own one someday.
