VOLUNTEER MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

Chicktech is a local nonprofit organization dedicated to getting females of all ages engaging with technology. As the organizaion has grown they had a big need for a a management system to keep their volunteers organized and give them more accountability within the organization.

I had the pleasure of interning with them to deliver them the custom webapp they needed to continue to grow

FEATURES

  • ・user sign up/log in
  • ・Roles: admin, superadmin, and volunteer
  • ・private email invites[mailer]
  • ・Create,Read,Update,Delete[CRUD] events
  • ・CRUD jobs and tasks
  • ・sign up at team leader or just commit to completing a job
  • ・mark jobs as done, and leave notes when neccessary

TECHNOLOGY USED

  • ・Ruby
  • ・Rails
  • ・Devise gem
  • ・CanCabn gem
  • ・Bootstrap
  • ・AJAX
  • ・jQuery
  • ・FactoryGirl
