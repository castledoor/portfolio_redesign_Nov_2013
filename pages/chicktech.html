VOLUNTEER MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

Chicktech is a local nonprofit organization dedicated to getting females of all ages engaging with technology. As the organizaion has grown they had a big need for a a management system to keep their volunteers organized and give them more accountability within the organization.

FEATURES

  • ・user sign up/log in
  • ・comment on posts
  • ・upvote on posts
  • ・posts sorted by vote count
  • ・count algorithm set to degrade over time

TECHNOLOGY USED

  • ・Ruby
  • ・Rails
  • ・Devise gem
  • ・Polymorphism [comment on comments]
  • ・AJAX
  • ・Heroku
